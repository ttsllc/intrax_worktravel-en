An Adventure You’ll Cherish—Work and Explore American Culture!

Why visit the U.S. as a tourist when you can dive deep into American culture while earning money and gaining valuable work experience? With the Work Travel Program, you’ll have the chance to work and travel in the U.S. for up to 4 months during your summer vacation. What’s in it for you?

  • Earn Money While Exploring: Work at exciting U.S. companies and earn to cover your expenses while discovering new places and meeting new people.
  • Travel the U.S. After Work: Once your job is done, enjoy a month of travel throughout the country to see iconic landmarks and local gems.
  • Improve Your Communication Skills: Gain practical language experience that will enhance your communication abilities.

Length: Minimum of 6 weeks during your Summer or Spring break. Enrollment ends once capacity is reached.

  • Application Support

  • Interview Assistance

  • Online Orientation to prepare you for your U.S. adventure

Requirements

To be eligible for the J-1 Summer Work Travel Program, you must:

  • Be currently enrolled in a university or four-year college and have completed at least one semester.
  • Be between 18 and 28 years of age.
  • Have a conversational level of English.
  • Be an independent and mature traveler.
  • Have a flexible attitude and willingness to learn.
  • Be committed to fulfilling job duties for the entire program duration.
  • Have at least $1,000 USD available for initial living expenses in the U.S.

Program Highlights

  • Work seasonally in the U.S. for up to 4 months
  • 24/7 support and emergency line for assistance in the US
  • Complete visa and paperwork support (J-1 visa and DS-2019 form)
  • Pre-departure orientation and on-the-ground support
  • Help with housing and travel arrangements
  • Guidance for obtaining your Social Security number
  • Access to a participant handbook and online portal

How It Works
